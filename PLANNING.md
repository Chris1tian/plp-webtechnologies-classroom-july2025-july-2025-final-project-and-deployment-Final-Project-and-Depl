# Website Planning

## Purpose
A portfolio website to showcase web development skills, projects, and contact information.

## Pages
1. Home
2. About
3. Projects
4. Contact
5. Gallery

## Layout Sketch/Description
- **Header:** Site logo/name, navigation menu
- **Footer:** Copyright, social links
- **Home:** Welcome message, highlights
- **About:** Bio, skills, education
- **Projects:** List of projects with descriptions and links
- **Contact:** Contact form, email, social media
- **Gallery:** Images/screenshots of work

## Navigation
- Header navigation links to all pages
- Footer includes quick links and social media
- Internal links between project items and gallery
